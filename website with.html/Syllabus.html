Syllabus for B.Des / B.Sc in Fashion Design

Year Semester Major Subjects
Year 1 Sem 1 & 2 Design Foundation, Sketching, History of Art, Material Studies
Year 2 Sem 3 & 4 Pattern Making, Textile Science, Garment Construction, Illustration
Year 3 Sem 5 & 6 CAD (Computer Aided Design), Fashion Marketing, Draping, Knitwear
Year 4 Sem 7 & 8 Portfolio Development, Graduation Collection, Internship

Syllabus for Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B.FTech)

  • Production: Garment Construction, Apparel Quality Management, Production Planning.
  • Technology: Sewing Machinery, Textile Testing, Lean Manufacturing.
  • Business: Apparel Costing, Global Sourcing, Supply Chain Management.

Syllabus for MBA in Fashion Merchandising

Retail Management
Focuses on store operations, visual merchandising, and consumer behavior.
Brand Strategy
Covers luxury branding, marketing communication, and digital presence.
Supply Chain
Explores logistics, export-import documentation, and vendor management.
