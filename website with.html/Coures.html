  • **>Bachelor of Design (B.Des)
    The Bachelor of Design is our flagship four-year program designed for the bold visionaries of tomorrow. This course goes beyond simple garment making to explore the deep "why" behind every design, focusing heavily on design thinking, conceptualization, and creative problem-solving. Students immerse themselves in the history of art, visual aesthetics, and advanced digital tools to build a unique artistic voice. If you want to lead a creative team or start your own high-fashion label, this program provides the comprehensive foundation you need to transform a blank canvas into a cultural statement.

    **>Fashion Design (B.Sc/B.A.)
    Our Fashion Design degree offers a balanced, three-year intensive journey that bridges the gap between artistic flair and technical execution. This course is perfect for students who want a hands-on experience, focusing on the core pillars of the industry: pattern making, garment construction, and textile science. You will learn how to translate your 2D sketches into perfectly tailored 3D realities, mastering everything from fabric selection to the final stitch. By the end of this program, you will have a professional portfolio that demonstrates your ability to create market-ready collections.

Certificate Courses (3 Months – 1 Year) For those looking to rapidly enhance their skill set or pivot their career without a long-term academic commitment, our Certificate Courses provide the ultimate fast-track solution. These intensive, hands-on programs focus on high-demand industry skills such as Fashion Styling, Boutique Management, Draping, and Fashion Photography. Designed for working professionals, entrepreneurs, and creative enthusiasts alike, these courses strip away the fluff to provide practical, real-world training. Whether you want to launch your own fashion blog, manage a retail outlet, or master the art of the perfect photoshoot, our short-term courses empower you with professional expertise in a fraction of the time. Diploma in Fashion Design (1 Year) Our Diploma in Fashion Design is an intensive, skill-oriented program designed for those who want to jumpstart their career in the fashion industry. This one-year course strips away long-term academic theory to focus on the essential "core pillars" of design: sketching, pattern drafting, and garment construction. Students are introduced to the fascinating world of textiles, learning how different fabrics behave and how to sculpt them into stunning silhouettes. Perfect for beginners or those looking to launch their own boutique, this diploma provides the practical confidence needed to translate creative ideas into physical, wearable art in a fraction of the time. Advanced Diploma in Fashion Design (2-3 Years) The Advanced Diploma is a comprehensive professional program that bridges the gap between basic design and creative leadership. Spanning two to three years, this course delves significantly deeper into specialized subjects such as Fashion Illustration with CAD, Trend Forecasting, and Luxury Brand Management. Beyond mastering complex sewing and tailoring techniques, students learn the strategic "business of fashion"—from merchandising and retail strategies to building a signature portfolio that meets international standards. With a heavy emphasis on industry exposure through workshops and exhibitions, this program prepares you for prominent roles such as Senior Designer, Fashion Buyer, or an Independent Entrepreneur.
